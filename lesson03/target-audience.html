Target Audience

Chill Earth is targeted at a wide range of people (teens to adults) who enjoy outdoor activities and want to stay updated on good activities for the current weather.

Personas

  • Taylor (16) loves outdoor sports and usually takes charge of planning activities with his friends. He's looking for some new ideas for activities to plan.
  • Jane (24) is fascinated by extreme weather. She loves lightning storms and blizzards. She wants a website that can help her find some good storms to enjoy.

Scenarios

  • Taylor and his buddies are going snowboarding. He wants to plan ahead, so he checks Chill Earth for the weather conditions at his favorite snowboarding resort.
  • Jane wants to spend a weekend on the beach in California. She checks Chill Earth to see when the next storm is so she can plan a good time for beach combing.
  • Taylor is in the mood for a bike ride up the canyon, but it's been a hot week. Luckily, Chill Earth shows that there's a good cloudy day later in the week.
  • Jane's crush seems like boyfriend material and she wants to ask him on a date. It's winter in Rexburg, so she looks up the next blizzard on Chill Earth, which would conveniently give her a good excuse to snuggle up with her boy and drink some hot choccy.
  • Taylor is a pretty good disc golfer and he's looking for a way to challenge himself. Chill Earth has some good recommendations for disc golf courses that typically have high winds.
