Welcome to Salt Lake City!

Salt Lake City was founded in 1847 by the Pioneers of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, who named the city after the Great Salt Lake. Today, Salt Lake City has a population of around 200,000 people. The city is part of an area nicknamed “Silicon Slopes” due to its growing technology industry, and it is a great place to build your startup business.
